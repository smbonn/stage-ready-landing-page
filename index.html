Scored Interview

The interview is a competitive category with real weight. You start with 26 role and behavioral questions from day one, then 75 more generated from your paperwork. Structure and confidence matter as much as content.

Upload: application, platform essay, or CSI paperwork

Miss USA / Miss Teen USA
Panel Format

Panel-format, opinion-based rounds. Two to three minutes, round-robin delivery. Miss USA winners advance to Miss Universe. The goal: hold a position, think on your feet, and never hedge.

Upload: bio, personal statement, platform info
Categories: Personal, Current Events, Role & Behavioral
Coaching focus: conviction and point-of-view under pressure
Miss Volunteer America
S.E.R.V.E. Framework

A 10-minute interview covering the S.E.R.V.E. framework plus 10 job competencies. Judges receive your paperwork in advance — they already know your story. Closing statement practice is built in and treated as seriously as the questions.

Upload: resume-style paperwork, S.E.R.V.E. summary, or platform essay
Categories: Personal, Community & Issues, Competencies
Partners: St. Jude, Salvation Army, American Red Cross
Teen Competitors
Personality-Driven

Teen interviews are conversational and personality-driven. Authenticity beats polish. Sierra's coaching for teen competitors focuses on letting the real person come through — not sounding like a rehearsed answer.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, information generated through your use of the app, and limited technical information.
